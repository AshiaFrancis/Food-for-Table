About Food for Table

Our Purpose

Originating in the local town of Norcross, Food for Table started with
one girl, and a desire to share all of her favorite restaurants with her
community. Ashia Francis, the creator of Food for Table, started out
with just an idea: What if we gave our community a way to view places they
would have never been? And so, Food for Table was born. As the years have
gone by, our purpose has become more focused on helping the Atlanta community
discover family owned restaurants and hidden gems, all over the Metro
Atlanta area.
